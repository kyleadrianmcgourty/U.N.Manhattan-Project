# U.N.Manhattan-Project
Since time immmemorial, humans, and likely lower species, have dreamed of living again after death. As a common humanity, what can we do to ressurect our first human? This is a moonshot, such that ressurcting humans may become possible for our kind.


As surgery was new to the 1600's, so likewise is the notion of humanity working to bring human ressurection into practice.

Many unknowns and U.N.knowns will be faced, but so long as humanity works in collarboration, our time will be well spent, honoring those who came before, those who are, and those who shall be.

This is an open repository, such that collaboration is welcome from all individuals.
